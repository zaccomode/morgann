ABOUT MELONS

Melons are the currency you require to purchase and send cards to people on your server. You passively gain ~1üçâ a minute, and can spend them on purchasing cards.

Purchasing cards will not send the recipient Melons, but instead void them.

Melons are server-specific.

About Reputation

Reputation, like Melons, are a form of currency passively gained at a rate of ~1 a minute, however are slightly different in functionality.

Unlike Melons, Reputation is affected when you are given a card: positive cards will increase your Reputation, and negative cards will do the inverse.

Also unlike Melons, Reputation is capped at 1000 to stop people from becoming too powerful.

Reputation is server-specific
