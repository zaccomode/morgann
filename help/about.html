Who's Morgann?

Morgann is a bot created by Isaac Shea to insult his friends automatically, and now you can do the same to yours!


Getting Started:

Simply add Morgann to your Discord server and brush up on the help pages, and you're set!

Interaction

Summoning:

Morgann is not like other bots who use a prefix (like ! or ?) to get their commands working. Morgann instead responds to his name, and a couple variations, and his command syntax is a bit more flexible than most bots'.


Commands

You can place a command to Morgann anywhere in a sentence and he should understand it!
